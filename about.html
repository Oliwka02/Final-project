Our Story

Welcome to the captivating narrative of Book Freaks, a literary haven born from the passion of three visionary minds. Founded in 2018 by Joyce M. Travis, a dynamic Vice President, Catherine C. Legrand, a visionary CEO, and Charles D. Batista, an innovative Chief Financial Officer, Book Freaks emerged in the heart of intellectual curiosity – a fusion of literature and technology. United by a shared love for the written word, our founders envisioned a space where diverse minds could converge, explore, and celebrate the boundless worlds that literature unfolds. Join us on this enchanting journey through the pages of our story, where literature meets innovation, and the spirit of Book Freaks thrives.
